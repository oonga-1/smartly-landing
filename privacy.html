Smartly AI

Homework assistance app

Age Requirement

Smartly AI is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.

Information We Collect

Device Identifier: We collect and store a unique device identifier on our secure servers solely to prevent abuse and ensure fair usage of our service. This identifier is not linked to any personal information.

User-Submitted Reports: If you choose to report an AI response for inaccuracy or other issues, the question you submitted may be uploaded to our servers for review. These reports do not contain personal identifiers beyond the content of the question itself.

Local Data: Your questions, uploaded photos, and response history are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload or have access to this data unless you submit a report.

How We Use Your Information

We use the device identifier to prevent abuse and ensure service availability for all users. Reported questions are used solely to improve the accuracy and quality of our AI responses.

Third-Party Services

Smartly AI uses third-party APIs to process your questions and generate responses. When you submit a question, it is sent to third-party servers for processing.

Data Storage and Security

Device identifiers and reported questions are stored securely on third-party infrastructure. We implement reasonable security measures to protect this information from unauthorized access.

Analytics and Advertising

We do not use third-party analytics tools. We do not display advertisements in this app.

Your Choices

Since we collect minimal data that is not personally identifiable, we do not offer a data deletion process. Your local history can be cleared directly from your device at any time.

Outspoken

Speaking confidence & voice coaching app

Information We Collect

Device Identifier: We generate a random, anonymous identifier stored locally on your device to manage rate limits and app functionality. This identifier is not linked to your name, email, Apple ID, or any personally identifiable information.

Audio & Video Recordings: When you use the recording feature, your device captures audio and video. These recordings are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to our servers or shared with any third party.

Speech Transcripts: Your device transcribes your speech locally. The resulting text transcript is sent to a third-party AI service for analysis (see "How We Analyze Your Speech" below). Only the text transcript is sent — never your audio or video.

Local Data: Your scores, progress, streaks, recordings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload or have access to this data.

How We Analyze Your Speech

To generate your speaking score, Outspoken sends the text transcript of your speech to a third-party AI processing service. Only the text transcript is sent — never your audio, video, or any personal information. The third-party service processes the transcript to return a score and feedback. We do not store your transcripts on any server. The third-party service does not use your data to train AI models.

To power the AI conversation practice feature, your typed messages are sent to the same third-party AI service to generate responses. These messages are processed in real time and are not stored on any server after the conversation ends.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not create user accounts. We do not track you across other apps or websites. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way.

Third-Party Services

Outspoken uses a third-party AI service to analyze speech transcripts and generate conversation responses. This service receives only text data, processes it in real time, and does not retain it. No audio, video, or personal information is shared with any third party.

Outspoken uses Apple's StoreKit framework to process subscriptions. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple and is never accessible to us.

Data Storage and Security

All app data — recordings, scores, progress, and preferences — is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Analytics and Advertising

We do not use third-party analytics tools. We do not display advertisements in this app.

Age Requirement

Outspoken is intended for users of all ages. We do not collect any personally identifiable information from any user, including children under 13.

Your Choices

Since we do not collect personally identifiable data or maintain user accounts, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete on our end. All data lives on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.

Photopin

Photo mapping & social sharing app

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in using Apple or Google, we collect and store your name, email address, a unique user ID, and your account creation timestamp. This information is used to create and manage your account.

Photos: When you upload images, they are stored on our servers and associated with your account.

Location Data: We collect and store the geographic coordinates associated with photos you upload in order to display them on a map. This data is tied to your account and is required for core app functionality.

Group Data: If you join or create groups, we store group membership and related data so you can view shared photos and maps with other users.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and display your photos on a map
  • Enable group features and sharing with other users
  • Provide core app functionality

Third-Party Services

Photopin also uses Apple and Google authentication services for account sign-in. These services provide basic account information such as your name and email address.

Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored securely on third-party infrastructure (Supabase). We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Data Sharing

Your photos and associated location data may be visible to other users within groups you join or create. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Your Choices

You may request to delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email listed below.

Age Requirement

Photopin is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
