Smartly AI

Homework assistance app

Age Requirement

Smartly AI is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.

Information We Collect

Device Identifier: We collect and store a unique device identifier on our secure servers solely to prevent abuse and ensure fair usage of our service. This identifier is not linked to any personal information.

User-Submitted Reports: If you choose to report an AI response for inaccuracy or other issues, the question you submitted may be uploaded to our servers for review. These reports do not contain personal identifiers beyond the content of the question itself.

Local Data: Your questions, uploaded photos, and response history are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload or have access to this data unless you submit a report.

How We Use Your Information

We use the device identifier to prevent abuse and ensure service availability for all users. Reported questions are used solely to improve the accuracy and quality of our AI responses.

Third-Party Services

Smartly AI uses third-party APIs to process your questions and generate responses. When you submit a question, it is sent to third-party servers for processing.

Data Storage and Security

Device identifiers and reported questions are stored securely on third-party infrastructure. We implement reasonable security measures to protect this information from unauthorized access.

Analytics and Advertising

We do not use third-party analytics tools. We do not display advertisements in this app.

Your Choices

Since we collect minimal data that is not personally identifiable, we do not offer a data deletion process. Your local history can be cleared directly from your device at any time.
